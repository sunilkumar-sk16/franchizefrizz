HarryPotter - The Boy Who Lived

Harry James Potter was an English wizard, and one of the most famous wizards.
The only child and son of James and Lily Potter.
Harry being known as "The Boy Who Lived", as he was the only known survivor of the Killing Curse.
He became even better known in his early years for protecting the Philosopher's Stone from Voldemort, saving Ron's sister Ginny Weasley.
He encountered Voldemort and nearly sacrificed himself.

Hermione

Hermione Jean Granger was an English Muggle-born witch born to Mr and Mrs Granger.
She possessed a brilliant academic mind and proved to be a gifted student in almost every subject that she studied.
Hermione became an advocate for the better treatment of house-elves, forming the association S.P.E.W.
Hermione played a significant role in many other battles of the Second Wizarding War, she dedicated herself to eradicating old laws that were biased in favour of pure-bloods.


Ron

Ronald Bilius "Ron" Weasley was an English pure-blood wizard, the sixth and youngest son of Arthur and Molly Weasley.
He became close friends with Harry Potter and Hermione Granger.
Together, they made the Golden trio, they faced many challenges during their adolescence.
Ron also became a Gryffindor prefect and a Keeper on the Gryffindor Quidditch team at hogwards.
Later,Ron became an Auror and helped reform and revolutionise the Ministry of Magic.
